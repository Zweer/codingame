# Mean Max

[:link: Puzzle on CodinGame](https://www.codingame.com/multiplayer/bot-programming/mean-max)

**Level:** multi
**Topics:** Programming Fundamentals, Algorithms, Multi-agent, Simulation

![](//cdn.codingame.com/smash-the-code/statement/league_wood_04.png)

This is a **league based** puzzle.

For this puzzle, multiple versions of the same game are available. Once you have proven your skills on this first version, you will access a higher league and extra rules will be unlocked. 

## The Goal

Gather more water than your enemies

## Rules

The game is played on a circular map with 3 players, each spawning with a Reaper.  
Players move simultaneously and compete to gather water from the Wrecks of Tankers.  
  
**The map** 
* The game is played in a circular area of radius 6000.
**The Reapers** 
* The Reaper harvests 1 water per turn from each Wreck it finishes the turn in.
* Your Reaper can X Y THROTTLE to accelerate towards (x,y).
**Actions**  
  
Every turn, your Reaper must either: 
* X Y THROTTLE to accelerate towards (x,y)
* WAIT
  
The game is over once any player reaches 50 water or after 200 turns.

Victory Conditions

Have more water than your opponents at the end of the game.

Loss Conditions

You do not respond in time.

## Expert Rules

[![](https://i.imgur.com/fwocZo8.png) ](https://i.imgur.com/fwocZo8.png) 

[Credit: Orabig](https://i.imgur.com/fwocZo8.png)

Your Reaper has a radius of 400 and accelerates by THROTTLE/mass.  
At the end of each turn, friction is applied as v=v\*(1-friction); positions and velocities are rounded. 
* Reaper: mass = 0.5 friction = 0.2
The collisions are inelastic and the entire physics engine is detailed in the [ referee on github](https://github.com/CodinGame/MeanMax/blob/master/Referee.java).  
  
**Reaper** 
* To harvest a Wreck, the center of the Reaper must be in the Wreck at the end of the turn.
* If the Reaper is in multiple Wrecks, it will harvest 1 unit of water from each Wreck.
* If multiple Reapers are in the same Wrecks, all Reapers will always harvest 1 unit of water, even when only 1 unit remains.

## Game Input

Input for one game turn

First 3 lines: the integer score of each player in order of id (you are player 0)  
Next 3 lines: an integer (placeholder for the next leagues)  
Line 10: an integer unitCount  
Next unitCount lines: unitId, unitType, playerId, mass, radius, x, y, vx, vy, extra, extra2 
* Integer unitId: The unit's id
* Integer unitType: 0 (Reaper), 4 (Wreck)
* Integer playerId: id of the player for Reapers, -1 otherwise
* Double mass: Mass of the unit, -1 for Wrecks
* Integer radius: The radius of the unit
* Two integers x and y: The position of the unit
* Two integers vx and vy: The speed of the unit
* Integer extra: The quantity of water for Wrecks, -1 otherwise
* Integer extra2: Always -1 (placeholder for the next leagues)

Output for one game turn

One line for your Reaper: 
* X Y THROTTLE three integers with (X,Y) the direction of your acceleration and 0 ≤ THROTTLE ≤ 300
* WAIT
Two lines of: 
* WAIT (placeholder for the next leagues)
  
You may append text to your action, it will be displayed in the viewer.

Constraints

Response time per turn ≤ 50ms  
Response time for the first turn ≤ 1000ms

What is in store for me in the higher leagues?

The extra rules available in higher leagues are: 
* You will control more Looters, and will need to destroy Tankers in order to collect Water from their Wrecks.
* Tankers will fill up at Water Town and will have to be destroyed by your Destroyer.
* Your Destroyer will be able to throw grenades at the cost of rage generated by your Doof.
* Your Reaper will be able to leave tar on the map to disturb other Looters.
* Your Doof will be able to leave oil on the map to disturb other Looters.
